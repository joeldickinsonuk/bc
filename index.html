Blooming Circus Across Schools and Events

Magic workshops for schools

At Blooming Circus, we specialise in bringing the wonder of magic into schools, colleges, universities, and educational events across the UK. With years of experience, we’ve mastered the balance between fun and learning, ensuring that students not only enjoy themselves but also gain lasting skills and confidence.

Our magic workshops are designed to captivate and inspire. Led by professional magicians who perform at prestigious events, we reveal the secrets of magic in a way that is easy to understand and exciting to practise.

Students will learn tricks with cards, coins, and everyday objects - the kind of magic that impresses friends and family while building performance skills. Alongside the tricks, we also teach the essentials of showmanship, misdirection, and presentation.

These sessions are about more than just magic. Pupils develop concentration, problem-solving skills, creativity, and self-confidence. Every workshop is inclusive, engaging, and leaves students with a sense of achievement.

We don’t just teach magic - we share the excitement of performing, the joy of discovery, and the confidence that comes with stepping into the spotlight. Let Blooming Circus bring the wonder of magic to your school and create an unforgettable learning experience for your pupils.

Circus workshops for schools

For over a decade, Blooming Circus has delivered high-energy circus skills workshops for schools, colleges, and youth groups. Our aim is to encourage confidence, creativity, and teamwork through the exciting world of circus arts.

As accomplished performers with professional accolades, we bring authenticity, enthusiasm, and real-world expertise to every workshop. From juggling and plate-spinning to balance skills and performance tips, we engage students in ways that are fun, inclusive, and educational.

What sets us apart is our background in the performing arts. Having worked with countless schools, colleges, and universities, we know how to communicate with students of all ages in a way that motivates and inspires.

Circus skills are more than just techniques - they’re tools for personal growth. Our workshops build resilience, creativity, coordination, and confidence, offering life skills that extend far beyond the classroom.

With Blooming Circus, students don’t just meet teachers - they meet professionals who live and breathe these skills every day. Our passion, energy, and expertise make every session vibrant, memorable, and impactful.

Let us transform your next school event into a celebration of learning, laughter, and creativity.
