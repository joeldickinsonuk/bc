Introduction

Interactive entertainment for schools, events, weddings and parties across the UK

Blooming Circus brings together circus workshops, close-up magic, roaming entertainment and interactive show moments for a wide range of bookings.

Some clients need a structured full-school day, some need guest-friendly entertainment for a wedding, and some need something lively for a public event or children’s party.

Everything is designed to feel engaging for the audience and straightforward for the organiser.

Popular Bookings

Entertainment that adapts to the booking

Every booking needs a slightly different shape, so the entertainment is built around the setting, audience and flow of the day.

  • Schools: structured shows and workshops with clear timing and full participation
  • Events: roaming entertainment and visible activities that build atmosphere
  • Weddings: close-up magic and flexible guest entertainment that fits around the day
  • Kids parties: fast-paced magic and circus fun that keeps children engaged
