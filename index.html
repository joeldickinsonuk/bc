Introduction

Professional circus workshops, magic and interactive entertainment across the UK

We provide circus workshops, magic and interactive entertainment for schools, events and private bookings across the UK.

Most of our work is with schools, delivering full-day workshops that are structured, inclusive and easy to organise. Alongside that, we also provide entertainment for events, youth groups and private parties.

Everything is designed to run smoothly and keep people engaged.

Schools

Circus Workshops for Schools

Our school workshops are built around a simple idea — keep every child involved and make the day run smoothly from start to finish.

A typical day includes a high-energy show, hands-on workshops for each group, and a final performance where students can show what they’ve learned.

Sessions are adapted for different ages and abilities, so everyone can take part and feel like they’ve achieved something.

We bring all equipment, manage the structure of the day, and keep things moving so teachers don’t have to.
