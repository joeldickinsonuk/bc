Blooming Circus Wedding Entertainment

Wedding Entertainment Full of Wonder

Your wedding day is a magical occasion, and Blooming Circus is here to make it unforgettable with unique entertainment tailored to delight guests of all ages.

Our Services

1. Strolling Performers

Enchant your guests with our talented strolling performers who bring a touch of elegance and whimsy to your celebration. Whether it’s a stilt walker, juggler, or a roaming magician, our performers mingle seamlessly, creating memorable moments and smiles everywhere they go.

2. Face Painting

Transform your little (and big) guests into fantastical characters with our professional face painters. Using safe, high-quality paints, we create vibrant designs ranging from delicate flowers to daring superheroes - perfect for kids and the young at heart.

3. Balloon Modeling

Watch as colorful balloons twist into delightful shapes, from animals to custom creations. Our balloon modelers are masters of their craft, ensuring everyone leaves with a unique keepsake and a smile.

4. Circus Workshops

Bring people together with our fun and engaging circus workshops! Suitable for both kids and adults, these sessions teach juggling, plate spinning, diabolo tricks, and more. Perfect for breaking the ice or creating unforgettable entertainment during your reception.

Why Choose Blooming Circus?

  • Tailored Entertainment: We work closely with you to ensure our performances align perfectly with your wedding theme and schedule.
  • Family-Friendly Fun: From kids to grandparents, our acts are designed to entertain and captivate guests of all ages.
  • Experienced Performers: Our team consists of skilled professionals with years of experience in creating enchanting experiences.
  • Our performers are multi-skilled and you only pay for each person so a number of services can be supplied at affordable and competitive prices.
