Circus Workshops That Inspire

For over two decades, we’ve been delivering circus workshops in schools across the UK - bringing a fresh, exciting, and captivating approach to learning and play.

But what makes us different? Well, we’re not just circus workshop hosts, we are also performers with life long experience travelling the world performing shows, circus, magic and we’re passionate about out craft.

We take pride in creating safe, inclusive, and unforgettable experiences that inspire pupils of all ages. Every workshop is designed not only to entertain but to build confidence, teamwork, and creativity, leaving children with memories that last a lifetime.

Our fun, interactive sessions are led by professional circus performers who share their skills and passion in a way that engages, excites, and motivates. From juggling and balancing to plate-spinning and more, each activity is tailored to ensure every child achieves something special.

How a School Day Works

  1. Opening Show: The day begins with a fun, interactive performance of juggling, magic, and laughter to excite the whole school.
  2. Workshops by Class: Each class enjoys their own dedicated circus skills session with our engaging performers.
  3. Inclusive & Adaptable: Activities are tailored for age, ability, and group size - ensuring every child achieves something new.
  4. Grand Finale: The day ends with a student-led showcase where children perform what they’ve learned for teachers and parents.
