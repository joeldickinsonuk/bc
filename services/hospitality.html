Corporate & Hospitality Entertainment

Professional Close-Up Magic for VIPs, Sponsors & Guests

Blooming Circus also provides high-quality close-up magic for corporate hospitality, VIP lounges and professional events, delivering engaging, interactive entertainment that works comfortably in premium environments.

Relaxed, Professional Style

Led by Jay Rollins, our hospitality work focuses on natural, conversational close-up magic performed directly for small groups. The style is relaxed, inclusive and designed to enhance the atmosphere without interrupting conversation, food service or the flow of an event.

This makes it particularly well suited to:

  • Football and sports hospitality
  • Sponsor lounges and director areas
  • Corporate receptions and networking events
  • Conferences and premium client entertainment
  • Brand and partner events

Experienced in Football & Live Event Environments

Jay regularly performs in VIP football hospitality settings, where timing, professionalism and audience awareness are essential.

Performances are adapted to suit:

  • Pre-kickoff and halftime periods
  • Mixed groups of sponsors, guests and families
  • Busy, high-energy environments where attention must be earned rather than demanded

The aim is always to add value to the guest experience, create memorable moments, and support the overall professionalism of the event.

Subtle, Flexible & Audience-Aware

Hospitality magic works best when it feels effortless. Performances are:

  • Close-up and self-contained
  • Suitable for standing or seated guests
  • Easily paused or moved as required
  • Appropriate for both adults and families

Because the style is adaptable, it works equally well in corporate-only settings and in environments where families and younger guests may also be present.
