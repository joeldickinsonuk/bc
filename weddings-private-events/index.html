Introduction

Entertainment that fills the quiet moments without taking over

Weddings naturally have quiet periods — during photos, between courses, or while the room is being prepared.

The right entertainment fills those gaps without taking over.

We provide a mix of close-up magic, interactive elements and optional children’s entertainment that keeps guests engaged, relaxed and enjoying the day from start to finish.

Why Close-Up Magic Works

It fits around the day instead of forcing the day around it

Close-up magic fits into a wedding without needing a stage, setup or formal attention.

It works during drinks receptions, between courses and in the early evening — exactly when guests would otherwise be waiting.

It naturally brings people together, gives them something to talk about, and helps create a relaxed atmosphere across the whole room.

For adult guests, it adds something unexpected and memorable without interrupting the flow of the day.

For The Whole Day

Not just one moment

We don’t just “do a show” — everything is designed to fit around your schedule.

  • Close-up magic can run during drinks reception while photos are being taken
  • Table magic works between courses without disrupting the meal
  • Early evening entertainment keeps guests engaged before the party starts

The aim is simple — no awkward gaps, no downtime, no need for you to think about it.

Entertainment Options

Tailored to the size and style of your wedding

  • Close-up magic for adult guests
  • Interactive circus skills that work for all ages
  • Children’s magic to keep younger guests entertained
  • Face painting, balloons and giant bubbles as optional extras

For larger weddings, we can bring additional performers so everything runs at the same time rather than in sequence.

Packages

Flexible options for different wedding sizes

Single Performer

Ideal for smaller weddings. Close-up magic moving between guests, with optional children’s entertainment built in.

Two Performers

Covers more guests at once. One performer can focus on adults while the other keeps children entertained.

Full Team (3–4 Performers)

Best for larger weddings. Close-up magic, children’s entertainment and visual acts all running simultaneously.

Additional Options

Fire performance for an evening finale, or stilt walkers for outdoor drinks receptions or arrivals.

Why Couples Book

You don’t need to think about this — we handle it

Most couples aren’t just looking for entertainment — they want the day to run smoothly.

  • You don’t need to organise timings around us
  • We adapt to the flow of your day
  • Guests stay engaged without needing direction

It means you can focus on enjoying the day, knowing your guests are being looked after.
