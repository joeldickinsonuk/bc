Popular Skills Include

Our workshops are hands-on, inclusive, and designed to inspire. Popular skills include:

  • Plate Spinning - fast, fun, and perfect for all ages.
  • Diabolo - exciting tricks with this timeless circus prop.
  • Flower Sticks / Devil Sticks - simple to pick up, amazing to master.
  • Feather Balancing - a favourite for learning coordination.
  • Poi Spinning - creative movement with a cultural twist.
  • And so much more.

Each workshop is tailored to your group, ensuring everyone achieves and has fun.
