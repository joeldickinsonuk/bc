Scouts & Youth Groups

Circus Skills Workshops for Scouts & Youth Groups

High-energy, hands-on circus skills workshops designed for Scouts, Cubs, Beavers, Brownies, Guides and wider youth groups. Sessions are built around participation, teamwork and progression, giving every young person a chance to try real skills in a fun, supportive environment.

We provide structured sessions that work in halls, outdoor spaces or activity days, with everything adapted to age group and group size.

What We Do

What We Do

We deliver interactive circus skills sessions where every participant is actively involved from start to finish.

Activities are designed to be easy to access, fun to progress in, and rewarding at every level.

Circus Skills Activities

Circus Skills Activities

We bring a wide range of equipment so groups can rotate, explore and build confidence across different skills.

  • Diabolo
  • Flower sticks
  • Spinning plates
  • Juggling equipment
  • Balance and coordination challenges
  • Group games and skill-based activities

All activities are taught step-by-step so beginners can succeed quickly while more confident participants are still challenged.

Session Structure

Session Structure

Each session is flexible depending on your timetable and group size.

Typical format:

  • Introduction and demonstration of equipment
  • Rotating workshop stations with hands-on learning
  • Group challenges and skill progression
  • Optional mini performance or showcase at the end

We keep everyone active, moving and involved throughout.
